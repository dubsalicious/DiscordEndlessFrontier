__**Artifact Availability**__

**Artifact Shop Only**
All 1\*, 2\*, 3\*, 4\* artifacts, 18 5\* artifacts.
You need at least a 1-star artifact upgraded to +10 for 2-star artifacts to appear in the shop.
Please see the `A.List Tab` on the Datasheet <http://bit.ly/efsheet> for more details.

**Guild Shop Only**
Two 5-star artifact are Guild Shop only (Secret Book 2 & Assassin's Boots).

**Honor Shop**
The remaining 5-star and all 6-star artifacts can be obtained from the Honor Chest (5,000 <:hc:232967258769457152>).
*5-star Artifacts from the Artifact Shop __can't appear__ in Honor Chests.*

**Guild Shop Artifact Rotation**
Every week (Monday @ 00:00 UTC), there is a new set of 6-star and 5-star artifacts.
Each 6-star artifact cost 11,000 <:gc:233662366905008129>  and 5-star costs 4,000 <:gc:233662366905008129> 
Please see the `Guild Shop Tab` on the 2nd sheet <http://bit.ly/efsheet2> for more specific information.

__**Artifact Transing**__
5\* and 6\* artifacts can be transcended to improve their stats greatly. Once you have access to 20 artifacts of the specified level the option becomes available. It is a very expensive process and produces a random artifact of the requested level. See `!artifacttrans` for more information.

__**Guild Barracks**__

The guild barracks provides supporting units for guild battls. Initially only the 1\* units are available. Higher tier units can be permanently unlocked by an one time gem payment.
```
Race    Instructor        Tier 1       Tier 2          Tier 3         Tier 4
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Human   Hot-Blooded Xuan  Infantry     Heavy Infantry  Fire Bird      Musketeer
Elf     Hoyden Goku       Elf Archer   Elf Warrior     Poison Archer  High Elf Archer
Undead  Dark Admiral      Skeleton     Warlock         Ghost          Skeleton Warrior
Orc     Raptor Rider      Orc Fighter  Orc Hunter      Frost Mage     Orc Wing
```
Guild barracks unlocks are *not* lost upon leaving or changing guild.

**Training**

Instructors allow support units to train to higher levels:
```
Basic   5\* Instructor
Sr      6\* Sr Instructor
Sr+     6\* Sr Instructor T1
Sr++    6\* Sr Instructor T2
Sr+++   6\* Sr Instructor T3
Sr++++  6\* Sr Instructor T3 + 5\* Instructor Pet
```
Multiple instructors improve the rate at which units are trained.

__**Guild Battles**__

**Guild Wars**

Guild Wars are pvp battles versus other guilds with consistent seasonal ranking across server. For more information, see `!guildwar`

**Guild Raids**

Guild Raids are pve battles versus Raid Bosses which provide currency rewards and fragments of pets unobtainable otherwise. For more information, see `!guildraid`

**Guild Invasion**

Guild Invasion involves members fighting 1 vs 1 against Raid Bosses. If the boss is not killed, another member can resume the fight. Points are awarded based on the difficulty of the boss. Guilds are ranked on these points. See `!guildinvasion`.
